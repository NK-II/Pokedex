Battle Deck

A battle deck in the context of Pokémon consists of a selection of Pokémon chosen for their complementary abilities, types, and strategies. A battle deck can include a variety of Pokémon, such as offensive attackers, defensive tanks, and support Pokémon that offer unique abilities. The goal is to create a cohesive team that can adapt to different battle scenarios and take advantage of type advantages and strategies to win battles.

Pokémon stats define their battle capabilities, including **HP** (health), **Attack** (physical power), **Defense** (physical resistance), **Special Attack** (special moves power), **Special Defense** (special resistance), and **Speed** (move order).
